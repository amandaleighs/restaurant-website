About

At ResturantX we serve only the best and freshest quality foods. Located on the corner of Jam Juice Road and Candy Can Lane. We have been offering quality food and service for hundereds of years to hungry people everywhere. The resturant was the dream of two culinary students of Algonquin College and that dream has grown into something magical! People have travelled far and wide just to taste our delicious soups. We take pride in delivering healthy tasty meals to all our customers and will continue to do so for many more years to come.
